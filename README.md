SideQuest is a mobile app that helps people escape boredom by turning real life into a series of fun challenges.
Users can browse a list of “side quests” — activities like meeting new people, learning new skills, or trying something outside their comfort zone.

After completing a quest, users can upload photos to document their experiences and share their progress. The goal is to encourage people to explore, socialize, and make life more exciting.

Features:

📋 Side quest list with real-life challenges

📸 Upload photos as proof of completed quests

🎯 Encourages trying new things and breaking routine

🌍 Discover new experiences and meet people
